## 2026-03-10 — Chrome Tab Group Bug Is Fixed

The Chrome bug that broke the tab group API — creating or renaming groups
failed, and groups appeared unnamed and white — has been fixed in Chrome
146.0.7680.72.

If your groups were resetting to unnamed after Chrome restarts and your spaces were losing
all their tabs, they need to be renamed to match the spaces.

Upgrading Chrome will prevent this from happening again.
